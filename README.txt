Acme AirNav Solutions, Inc.  (Acme ANS, Inc.  for short) is a fictitious company that specialis-es
in helping airports manage their resources and coordinate operations through the devel-opment of
software solutions.  The goal of this project is to develop a WIS to assist airports in managing
logistics for charter flights.  This includes flight scheduling, booking management, crew
management, incident reporting and resolution, as well as aircraft maintenance.
